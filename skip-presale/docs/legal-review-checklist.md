# Legal Review Checklist

- Review jurisdictional restrictions and user eligibility.
- Review token sale language for investment, profit and listing claims.
- Review risk disclaimer placement and wording.
- Review treasury disclosure and refund mechanics.
- Review privacy implications of wallet analytics and event indexing.
- Review tax treatment and contribution records.
- Review marketing materials and social posts before publication.
- Do not imply regulatory approval, certification or government endorsement.
